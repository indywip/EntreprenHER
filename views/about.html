HERworld allows you as a business owner to promote your business or event by allowing you to create posts, videos, and livestream. Also on the website, you are given tools such as the ability to link your shop directly to the app to control sales and view payment details. You can also run inventory and see the most popular items brought from your store.

Using HERworld as a regular user allows you to find local businesses that are relevant to you. It also allows you to see posts and live streams from your favorite businesses as well as new ones. Also within the app, you will be able to track shipping and view your recent purchases.

We bring you closer to clients by eliminating all trends, instead we use fully randomized technologies on our discover page that do not favor anyone more than another.

We pride ourselves on treating you like a family member. We encourage you to learn and grow together to foster a more positive community.
